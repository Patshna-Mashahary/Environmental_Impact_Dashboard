# 🌿 Environmental Impact Tableau Dashboard

> An interactive Tableau dashboard visualizing carbon emissions, energy usage, 
> water consumption, and recycling rates across industries and regions.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes environmental impact data across **50,000 records** 
spanning multiple industries, regions, and product types. The goal is to 
support sustainability teams and compliance officers in making data-driven 
decisions to reduce environmental footprint.

**Business context:** Built based on a formal Business Requirements Document (BRD)
for a Tableau dashboard project.

---

## 🎯 Key Features

- **KPI Cards** — Total Carbon Emissions, Average Recycling Rate, 
  Total Energy Usage, Total Water Consumption
- **Bar Chart** — Carbon emissions comparison by region
- **Line Chart** — Energy usage trends over time
- **Heatmap** — Recycling rates by industry and region
- **Pie Chart** — Distribution of compliance levels (High/Medium/Low)
- **Scatter Plot** — Relationship between carbon emissions and recycling rates
- **Interactive Filters** — Region, Industry, Compliance Level, Product Type
